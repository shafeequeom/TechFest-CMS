Toc H Institute of Science and Technology

Toc H Institute of Science and Technology (TIST) is not merely another Engineering College producing hordes of Engineering graduates every year. It is more than a center of learning and home to a diverse community of innovators and entrepreneurs. In every classroom and laboratory, you’ll find unique people who are passionate about learning, teaching and collaborating on groundbreaking technologies.

TIST prepares future leaders through an innovative engineering education that bridges Science and Technology, enterprise and society. We inspire our students to indulge in research, innovation and excellance, so that they can gain valuable hands-on learning opportunities from real-world experiences. We aspire to be home to innovative minds making new discoveries, groundbreaking inventions, and top-flight graduates, all contributing to the strength of our economy and the health and vitality of our community.

Advay 2016

ADVAY ‘16 It began as a platform for the students to showcase their talent and knowledge in various fields of cultural significance in the year 2009. Since then ADVAY has come a long way to become one of the major festivals in Kochi. Students at TIST have always ventured beyond books and classrooms to inculcate ingenuity and innovation as part of college life. Every year, the otherwise cool and calm minds of Tistians bubble out their zest through the colors of ADVAY. Welcome to the 2016 version of this colorful extravaganza!! 50 colleges, over 50 events, over 20,000 footfalls! Be there to experience the rejuvenation felt like never before!!

With the choices of informal events, competitions and performances lined up at this year's Advay, where else would you be this February? Step into this wonderland on the 23rd & 24th of February 2016, and your life will never be the same again. Our aim is simple. Explore and Exhibit talent whatever it is.
